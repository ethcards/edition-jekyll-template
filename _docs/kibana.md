> 當有人入侵你網站時 Kibana Alert 會通知你

To share a video:

1. Open a message with someone
2. Select the **Send Media** button
3. Pick a video or paste a YouTube link

> Videos can use up a lot of bandwidth so be aware of your mobile data.
